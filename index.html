✦ FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Ladva Enterprises.

How long does a typical consulting engagement last?

+

Engagement lengths vary by scope. A focused strategy project typically runs 6–12 weeks. Ongoing retainer engagements run 3–12 months. Larger transformation programs can last 12–24 months. We define a clear timeline and milestones at the start of every engagement.

Do you work with startups or only large enterprises?

+

We work with businesses of all sizes — from seed-stage startups to large listed enterprises. Our Starter package is specifically designed for SMEs and growing businesses that need focused advisory without the overhead of large consulting firms.

What industries does Ladva Enterprises specialize in?

+

We have deep expertise across manufacturing, financial services, technology, healthcare, retail, real estate, and professional services. Our consultants bring industry-specific knowledge to every engagement, avoiding generic one-size-fits-all approaches.

Is the first consultation really free?

+

Yes, absolutely. Every engagement begins with a complimentary 60-minute discovery call with a senior consultant. We use this session to understand your challenges, assess fit, and give you immediate, actionable insights — no commitment required.

How do you measure and report on results?

+

We define clear, measurable KPIs at the start of every engagement. Clients on our Growth and Enterprise packages get access to our real-time client portal, with monthly performance reports and quarterly board-level presentations. We hold ourselves accountable to outcomes, not outputs.

What makes Ladva Enterprises different from other consulting firms?

+

Three things set us apart: (1) We don't use templates — every strategy is built from scratch for your context. (2) We stay through execution, not just advisory. (3) We have a 98% client satisfaction rate and measure our success by your ROI, not our billable hours.
